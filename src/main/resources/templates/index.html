About us

What is Bhakundo Mania?

Bhakundo Mania is an online platform that allows players, teams, and organizations to book bhakundo courts for various activities such as training, friendly games, tournaments, and leagues. The system typically offers a variety of features that make it easy and convenient for users to search, book, and pay for their preferred bhakundo courts..

Bhakundo Mania provides an easy and convenient way for players, teams, and organizations to find and book bhakundo courts, reducing the hassle and time required to book courts through other means. It can also help court owners or administrators to better manage their court listings and maximize their bookings and revenue..
